About Us

AFTER MORE THAN TWO DECADES OF FINE TURNING, WE HAVE WHAT YOU SEE TODAY

We are continuesly focused on bringing our guests globally inspired creations using high-quality ingredients. The result is a menu of bold global fusion flavours you are sure to crave. While we pride ourselves on creativity, we never lose sight of serving warm, tasty comfort, and signature food. Situated on Tinubu street, south of obasanjo house in the hearth of a populated upper-middle-class neighbourhood, the concept was simply to create a cozy environment that was well suitated to have great conversation, served quietly freshly cooked meals and offered great draft beer and spirit selections.

COOKING IS A LOT LIKE LIFE. NOT EVERYTHING IS GOING TO WORK, BUT IF YOU NEVER TRY YOU WILL NEVER KNOW!
